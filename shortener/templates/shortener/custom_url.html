How to Set Up Your Custom Domain with TinyURL.run

Follow these simple steps to start using your own domain for custom short URLs

1

Choose Your Custom Domain

Select a memorable subdomain for your custom URL shortener. Popular choices include links.yourbrand.com, go.yourbrand.com, or short.yourbrand.com.

Domain Naming Best Practices:
  • Keep it short and memorable
  • Use action words like "go" or "visit"
  • Maintain brand consistency
  • Avoid special characters or numbers
2

Add Domain to TinyURL.run

Log into your TinyURL.run Pro account and navigate to Custom Domains. Add your chosen domain and we'll provide specific DNS instructions.

What You'll Need:
  • TinyURL.run Pro account ($12/month)
  • Access to your domain's DNS settings
  • Your chosen subdomain name
3

Configure DNS CNAME Record

In your domain registrar's DNS panel, create a CNAME record pointing your subdomain to redirect.tinyurl.run. This usually takes 5-10 minutes to propagate.

DNS Configuration:
Type:
CNAME
Name:
links (or your chosen subdomain)
Value:
redirect.tinyurl.run
4

Verify & Start Creating URLs

Once DNS propagates, we'll automatically verify your domain and issue an SSL certificate. You can then start creating custom short URLs with your own domain!

You're Ready When:
  • Domain shows "Verified" status
  • SSL certificate is active
  • Custom URLs redirect properly
  • Analytics tracking is enabled
